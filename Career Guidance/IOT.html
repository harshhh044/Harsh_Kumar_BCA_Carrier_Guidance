NETWORKING, IOT & EMERGING TECHNOLOGIES

Networking (Computer Networking):

Networking is the way computers and devices connect to share information, like how phones or laptops use Wi-Fi to access the internet or send messages.

IoT (Internet of Things):

IoT means connecting everyday devices (like fans, lights, or watches) to the internet so they can collect and share data, work automatically, or be controlled remotely.

Emerging Technologies:

Emerging technologies are new and advanced tools or ideas (like AI, robots, or virtual reality) that are still growing but have the power to change how we live and work.

HOW THEY WORK?

Networking

Networking works by connecting computers and devices so they can share information. Data travels through cables or wirelessly using devices like routers and switches, following specific rules (called protocols) to reach the right destination.

IoT

IoT works by using smart devices with sensors to collect data (like temperature or motion), which is sent through the internet to apps or cloud systems. Users can then monitor or control these devices remotely using their phones or computers.

Emerging Technologies

Emerging technologies work by using new and powerful tools like AI, robotics, or virtual reality to solve problems, automate tasks, and improve how we live and work. They often rely on data, advanced software, and smart machines.

Networking
