Learning Roadmap

🎨 What is UI & UX Design?

User Interface (UI) design focuses on the look and feel of a product’s interface—buttons, icons, typography, color palettes. User Experience (UX) design ensures the product is intuitive, accessible, and provides a seamless journey for users.

📌 Importance & Usage

Great UI/UX reduces friction, increases user satisfaction, and drives engagement and conversions in digital products like websites, mobile apps, and software platforms.
